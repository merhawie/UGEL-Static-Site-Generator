article 96: Legislation remaining in force when the constitution comes into effect

<ul>
			<li>1 - : Laws and regulations which were in force on 1st April 1941, and have not since been repeated by the Administrating Authority and the laws and regulations enacted by that Authority, shall remain in force so long as they have not been repeated and to the extent that they have not been amended.<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>2 - : In the event of conflict between such laws and regulations and this Constitution, the Constitution shall prevail in accordance with Article 90 (3).<ul>
			</ul></li></ul>
