article 10: Laboratories for official and private checks
The yearly (3 month) full examination sample (organoleptic, physicochemical, undesirable and toxic substances, microbiological parameters) is to be collected by an official person and analyzed in an official laboratory.
The routinely-taken samples are to be collected by the management of the plant and analyzed in the in-plant laboratory or in an external private accredited laboratory. These examinations are to be carried out under the supervision of the official inspector.
