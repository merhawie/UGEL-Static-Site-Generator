article 8: Deprivation of Nationality

<ul>
			<li>1 - : A committee composed of the Secretaries of Justice, Internal Affairs and Public Administration may deprive of his nationality an Eritrean national by birth or an Eritrean granted Nationality by Naturalization pursuant to article 4 hereof, who has attained the age of eighteen and has capacity under the law where such a person:<ul>
						<li>a - : voluntarily acquires a foreign nationality after the publication of this Proclamation; or<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>b - : officially renounces his Eritrean nationality; or<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>c - : signs an oath of allegiance of another country after the publication of this Proclamation; or<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>d - : in violation of an explicit provision of Eritrean law, serves or continues to serve another country; or<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>e - : is condemned for treason by a court of law.<ul>
						</ul></li>			</ul></li>			<li>2 - : A committee composed of the Secretaries of Justice, Internal Affairs and Public Administration may deprive of his Nationality a person who has been granted Eritrean Nationality by Naturalization (articles 4 to 6) where such a person:<ul>
						<li>a - : acquires Eritrean nationality or a certificate of nationality by fraud, deceit or concealment of decisive facts; or<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>b - : is confirmed, that he has, by illegally contacting external powers, committed acts which aided and abetted an enemy;<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>c - : has committed treason outside Eritrea;<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>d - : has been indicted for a crime and sentenced to more than five years imprisonment;<ul>
						</ul></li>						<li>e - : has committed any one of the acts enumerated in sub article(l) hereof.<ul>
						</ul></li>			</ul></li>			<li>3 - : A person shall be deprived of his nationality pursuant to sub-articles (l) and (2) hereof only after the necessary investigation has been conducted and after such a person has been given an opportunity to defend himself.<ul>
			</ul></li></ul>
