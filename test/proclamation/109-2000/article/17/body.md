article 17: Maintenance of Books and Foreign Currency
Where a Contractor elects, which election shall be final, to maintain all books and records relating to Petroleum Operations in the currency of the United States of America:
<ul>
			<li>a - : The Income Tax Authority shall determine the Taxable Income or assessed loss attributable to Petroleum Operations for any year of assessment in that currency; and<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>b - : Notice of assessment and of any amount of tax payable shall be given to the taxpayer in that currency; and c) Payment of tax and refunds, if applicable, shall be effected in that currency.<ul>
