article 41: Inspection and Control

<ul>
			<li>1 - : The Department monitors that the requirements laid down in or under this Proclamation are complied with, including monitoring of the operator&#39;s contract with the user.<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>2 - : The Department may, in connection with the monitoring, carry out random tests and initiate measurements and other control measures. Control may be carried out without prior notice.<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>3 - : Anyone submitted to control measures shall ensure that the Department has unimpeded access to the premises where communications equipment or other devices subject to this Proclamation are located.<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>4 - : The Department may order the owner or the owner&#39;s representative to be present during the control. Necessary documentation shall be made available to the Department. Lack of co-operation during the control may result in closure of the applicable communications activities.<ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>5 - : The Department may order the establishment of internal control systems to ensure that the requirements laid down in or under this Proclamation are complied with Documentation showing the compliance of the requirements as regards internal control shall be prepared. This documentation shall be accessible to the Department.<ul>
			</ul></li></ul>
