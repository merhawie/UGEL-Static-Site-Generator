article 10: Areas Precluded from Petroleum Operations 

<ul>
			<li>1 - : The Minister shall, in consultation with the appropriate state organs, determine the areas in which Petroleum Operations may not be permitted for reasons of national interest and security; <ul>
			</ul></li>			<li>2 - : The determination made under sub-Article (1) of this Article shall be without prejudice to existing rights in such areas as established by a Petroleum agreement.<ul>
